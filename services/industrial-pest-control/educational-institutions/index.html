Educational Institutions

Educational Institutions

Schools, colleges and nurseries are particularly vulnerable to pest plagues, our pest control services for schools, colleges, nurseries, universities and local education authorities are ideal for eliminating and removing pests. we understand that the health and safety of your students and staff are vital in creating a dynamic learning environment. With a pest control and prevention programme, we can help to deliver the highest standard of health and safety, including the protection of staff and student welfare, preventing damage to buildings, eliminating risk of closure, and preventing contamination of food areas.

The common areas such as cafeteria, library, hall and other facilities are ideal places for pests to thrive with ample nesting areas and food. Early detection and continuous monitoring are essential to minimise the risk of pest infestation.

we can make sure your educational institution is pest-free for the long-term. We offer our integrated Pest Management approach to control pests without unnecessary chemicals. Our pest treatments are designed to handle all of your pest control needs so you can focus on what matters most – your students and their development.
