With the huge amount of investment, sweat, and tears you build homes and buildings in your life, a termite infestation can damage and be overwhelming. The colonies of termites work 24 hours. We would not notice the termite infestation until serious damage is caused to the structure. They do not directly attack people or animals. Instead, they cause severe structural damage and will most likely ruin the aesthetic of a beautiful house or building
if all conditions were made perfect. Therefore, it is important to prevent access to termites in our homes and office/ industrial buildings.

There are various methods available to control termites, such as physical barriers, chemical barriers, and termite baits, which can be effectively combined to gain optimum results as one of the market leaders in the pest control industry, Pesticide Guard termite control offers a number of solutions that are specially and specifically designed for different termite species and customer’s requirement.

Our comprehensive Eco-Friendly Termite Pest Control Doha inspection and treatment types are backed by Pesticide Guard Termite Warranty. Throughout, the years, many property owners and industries alike have entrusted Pesticide Guard to deal with their termite problems.

Fact #1

Termites are highly organized insects in the Isoptera order. They form large, strong colonies with queens, kings, workers and soldiers, all of them with their assigned duties.

Fact #2

The colonies of termites work 24 hours. We would not notice the termite infestation until serious damage is caused to the structure.

PREVENTION TIPS

  • Seal the property, particularly doors and windows
  • Repair leaking taps and pipes
  • Mop up moisture
  • Regularly inspect wood furniture and floors
  • Regularly check that termite shielding is not damaged or breached in any way

HOW DOES OUR TERMITE TREATMENT WORK?

STUDY

Pesticide Guard termite control experts will cautiously undertake an assessment in and around your property or work premises to uncover the intensity of infiltration. once a full evaluation finished, a recommendation will be made on the option of the treatment. we will then effectively treat active termites found and in case there’s no evidence of an infestation, we will implement a proactive termite prevention solution.

TREAT THE TARGET PEST

Considering the experts recommendation, the method shall be applied such as liquid termite treatment in which drilling will be done along the wall joints of the tiles, cemented, concreted and terrazzo floor with 3-5 feet intervals. Then the termicides will be injected to the soil underneath the floor. In the termite bait stations method, the termite bait cartridges will be used which contain the latest insect growth regulator which provides fast eradication of subterranean termite infestations. The bait attracts termites to the bait stations to feed even when there is a plentiful supply of alternative foodstuff in the area.

Further, Fumigation is the most effective of termite treatments, termite fumigation involves preparing and tenting your home, and filling the tent with fumigant that penetrates deep into the wood, wiping out the infestation.

OBSERVE THE POST TREATMENT SITUATION

After carrying out appropriate termite treatment, our technician may have extra visits as per the guarantee term and observe the existence and situation of post treatment.

PREVENTIVE TIPS

At the end, our well-trained staff will give you some useful advice and tips on how to avoid bedbug’s invention in the future in your premises and entity along with a detailed report on the service done.
